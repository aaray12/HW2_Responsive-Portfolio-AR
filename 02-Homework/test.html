About My Cat

This is my cat, one of two. Her name is Wh, and she is weird and bitchy, but I love her. She loves me, and being given food, and licking things. She tolerates my wife, and the other cat (unless the other cat wants to play, in which case she looses her mind with incandescant fury).

She is 16-and-a-half-years old, which is old enough to be allowed certain eccentricities. She is old enough now that her jet black coat is dusted with patches of white, and I can no longer remember why I named her "Wh".

A couple of years ago one of her whiskers, previously as black as her fur, grew in white, instead. Last year, another one followed suite.
